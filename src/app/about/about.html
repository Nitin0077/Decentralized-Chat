About Decentralized Chat

Secure • Private • Decentralized

Decentralized Chat is a secure, private, and fast messaging platform built for people who value their privacy. Unlike traditional chat apps, your data is never stored on centralized servers, ensuring that only you and your chat partners have access to your messages.

With end-to-end encryption, peer-to-peer communication, and no central authority, Decentralized Chat gives you complete control over your conversations.

How We Built It

  • Frontend: Angular 20
  • Backend: Node.js
  • Real-time Communication: Socket.IO
  • UI Styling: CSS3 with Responsive Design
  • Image Preview: Lightbox Gallery
  • Storage: IndexedDB (Local Chat Storage)
  • Security: WebSocket Encryption
